CEO's And Business Owners

Our first point of contact, as well as the ongoing client relationship, is with company CEO's and Business Owners at Sponsoring Companies. Clients hand select aspiring Leader candidates from within their organizations for inclusion in our program. We work with companies of all sizes in the greater Kansas City area where the leader at the top is ready to make the extra investment in his/her leadership team's growth, capabilities, and effectiveness.

Sponsoring Company Rationale

Successful companies have aspiring Leaders that they want to invest in and develop. Adding an experienced coach and mentor to the mix can further a company's investment.
Companies will likely come across opportunities and challenges that we've already seen and handled effectively. Our mentorship draws from a deep well of corporate and life experiences.
Leaders appreciate interest in their career development. Strengthened relationships derived from investment in an executive can improve skills, commitment, and outcomes.
Leaders often need experienced, thoughtful, and intentional mentors to reach their full potential. Seasoned and experienced mentors can be a great asset to your team and your company.
This program provides a unique and collaborative way to make a big impact in the Kansas City community. As Water Stone, together with our Clients, direct their support to our Designated Charity, the impact is real and material.
